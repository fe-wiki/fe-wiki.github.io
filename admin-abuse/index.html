Admin Abuse

This page contains information that may be inaccurate due to the ever-changing nature of how Admin Abuses are conducted.
This page talks about controversal developer-run events.

Admin abuses are hosted every week by the developers of the game. They occur at 12:00 PM Eastern Standard Time on Saturday and 8:00 AM Eastern Standard Time on Tuesday. Admin abuses give rare clovers and items, and rolling boosts.

Items given in the past include Clover Compressors, Enchant Mergers, Clover Packs, Merchant Tickets, and Red Envelopes (gives one of the 420M, 500M, 1B, and 1.5B enchants at a 1/100 rate).
