Achievements

Achivements give buffs for rolling or inventory space for certain goals reached in Dimension 1.

Objective Buff
Gain a total amount of RNG Gives Luck.
Roll an amount of times Gives Roll Speed.
Gain a total amount of Variant RNG Gives Bulk Rolls.
Play for an amount of minutes Gives Inventory Space.
Obtain an amount of RNG from Secrets Gives Secret Luck.
Roll an Enchant Orb with an amount of Enchants Gives Enchant Luck.
