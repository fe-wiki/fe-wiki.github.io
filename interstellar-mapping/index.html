Interstellar Mapping

Interstellar Mapping is an upgrade tree avaliable in Dimension 2 using Anti-Interference Index as currency.

Upgrade Description Cost WIP
Multiply Gives +1 Bulk Roll in Dimension 2. There are 6 Multiply upgrades avaliable.
Fortune Gives +100% Luck in Dimension 2. There are 7 Fortune upgrades avaliable.
Wisdom Gives +7.5% Secret Luck in Dimension 2. There are 4 Wisdom upgrades avaliable.
Twilight Gives +5% Variant Luck in all Dimensions. There are 4 Twilight upgrades avaliable.
Haste Gives +7.5% Rolling Speed in Dimension 2. There are 7 Haste upgrades avaliable.
Expansion Gives +3 Inventory Slots. There are 7 Expansion upgrades avaliable.
Radioactive Volatility Increases clover luck by 50%, 75% and 100%.. There are 3 Radioactive Volatility upgrades avaliable.
Radiation Compound Allows you to use two clovers at once if the two clovers are the same variant. Gives +50% luck for the second clover.
Ionizing Radiation Gives +1000%/2000%/3000%/4000% luck for two minutes upon using a Mega/Giga/Nexus/Titanic clover. 3
Unfortunate One's Pity Reduces luck by 25% but gives +75% for every aura rolled under 200 RNG. Resets when a 200 RNG or more aura is rolled. 6
