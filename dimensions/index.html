Dimensions

there are two dimensions currently in-game.

Dimension 1

Dimension 1 is the main dimension in-game. You can use and obtain clovers here.

Merchant

the Merchant sells clovers and other items here, such as fruits, tickets, clover compressors, and enchant mergers, in exchange for clover shards.

Clover Shard Invest

the Clover Shard Invest allows you to use clover shards in order to get buffs to your Merchant. Buffs are:

Buff Description
Free Restock Gives one free restock every hour avaliable by going near the character next to the merchant.
Purchases Return Gives a small amount of clover shards back based on items you buy at the merchant.
3 Merchant Slots Allows 3 more items to spawn in the merchant every restock.
Random Discount Random items in your stock will be on discount for around half the price.
Shared Return What does tdis do?
4 Merchant Slots Allows 4 more items to spawn in the merchant every restock.
Enchant Merger Allows Enchant Mergers to be stocked in the merchant.

Clover Scavenger

Sells clovers tdat you voided by not picking up at a price.

Follower / Like / Favorite Boards

Get clovers every hour from following developers and favoriting the game. Also shows when favored / supporter clover rains will occur.

Achievements

Get buffs while in Dimension 1 from completing achievements.

Dimension 2

Dimension 2 a.k.a. "The Moon" is the second dimension in-game. You can not use clovers here.

Interstellar Mapping

Interstellar Mapping allows you to get upgrades for rolling in Dimension 2 and also some upgrades for Dimension 1.
