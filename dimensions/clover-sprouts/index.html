Clover Sprouts

Clover Sprouts spawn in a similar way to clovers, wherein they have a chance of spawning in places where clovers can spawn.
The chance of a Clover Sprout spawning is 0.4% per minute with a pity system in place increasing the chance.

Clover Sprouts spawn with 1x luck and 1x variant luck. In order to start the sprout growth, roll near the sprout. It should start a countdown where the sprout will increase in luck and variant luck, and possibly obtain a variant.

After the sprout's coutdown ends, a number will appear with the health of the sprout. Rolling RNGs removes health from the sprout and also possibly grows the sprout. You'll get boosts to your roll based on the variant and luck of the sprout, showing in the same way a clover would.

Once the HP is depleted, the sprout will no longer grow and will despawn in a minute or two.

Fun Facts

  • Clover Sprouts currently aren't in-game as of Feb 24 due to them being too overpowered. They will maybe return at a later date.
  • The clover sprout can gain variants mid-growth, although it is unclear when this happens.
  • The sprout gave luck multipliers up to x100000 before the nerf.
