Items

Items can be accessed through your Luck Index. There are many types of items.

Clovers

Clovers can be used for one-roll boosts. They multiply your luck by the number given on the clover. They also give a variant if displayed on the clover.

There are also different types of clovers, which each do unique things.

Type Description
Undead This clover has a 2/3 chance to recycle on use, allowing it to be used again.
Precise This clover gives RNGs rounded to the nearest tenths place, allowing half-secrets to be rolled easier.
Rounded This clover gives RNGs rounded to the nearest ones place, allowing half-secrets to be rolled easier.
Grand This clover gives its buff to everyone in your server.
True This clover gives its buff to everyone, globaly.
Random This clover gives random variants on roll, with the variants given being affected by the variant on the clover.
Degrading This clover degrades its variant over uses.

Tickets

Tickets allow you to restock your merchant. There are 3 types of tickets.

Type Description
Normal Restocks your merchant normally.
Golden Restocks your merchant with 25x luck for enchant mergers, clover compressors, anti-interference indexes, etc. Doesn't affect clover luck as far as I am aware.
Sapphire Restocks your merchant with only clover shards.

Curriences

Curriences can be used to buy things from various places. There are a few different curriences shown in the items.

Type Description
Clover Shards Used to buy items from the merchant or clover scavenger. Can be obtained from rolling and using clovers.
Anti-Interferece Index Used to upgrade your Interstellar Mapping. Can be obtained from indexing in Dimension 2 and from the merchant.

Fruits

Fruits are boosts which last 7 minutes. They can be bought from the merchant.

Fruit Buff
Strawberry Gives +2 bulk.
Blueberry Gives +50% roll speed.
Mango Gives +50% variant luck.
Avocado Gives +300% luck.
Grape Gives +50% secret luck.
Ultimate Smoothie Gives all fruit buffs, in one. They are very rare to obtain.

Usables

There are a lot of other items that don't fit into any other category so they will be listed here.

Item Description
Clover Compressor Compresses 10 clovers into a better clover by selecting one for the variant and another for the type. If both selected are the same, you'll get a crazy multiplier clover.
Enchant Merger Merges two enchants together.
Portals Portals to D1 and D2 can be rarely obtained from the merchant.
Rarity Orb Given when you recieve an aura from a trade with your inventory full. Use to get that aura.
WIP More items can be obtained that aren't listed here yet.
